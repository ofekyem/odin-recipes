Cornflakes

Description

Cornflakes with milk is the easiest meal you can make yourself when you don't have much time or motivation to make something. you can actualy just make it in 1 minute and feel fullness and satisfied from it. so, let's make it!

Ingredients

  • a bowl
  • a spoon
  • milk
  • box of cornflakes

Steps

  1. Put some cornflakes from the box in your bowl.
  2. Add some milk to it.
  3. Eat it with your spoon.
